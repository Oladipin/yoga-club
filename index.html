our forms of yoga

yoga as exercise

A physical activity consisting mainly of postures, often connected by flowing sequences, sometimes accompained by breathing exercises and frequently ending with relaxation lying down or meditation.

yoga as therapy

Yoga therapy is a mind-body practice that focuses on your physical, emotional and mental health. The practice uses movement, mindfulness, meditation, relaxation and breathing exercises to help you relax, relieve stress and manage underlying conditions or symptoms.
